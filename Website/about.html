Bio

Going back to the very beginning I always found my self being fascinated by the beauty of nature combined with elegant art and designs. I knew I had to follow my inner compass showing me the direction of my passion which was aesthetics with a more earthly and natural aspect of creative proccess.

I was born in Seattle in the year 1985 and after finishing High School I studied interior design in Savannah College of Art and Design in Georgia with a focus on natural materials and psychology of space. These years were a verification of my instict of pursuing the combination of nature and design where I could express my creativeness.

The upcoming years I worked as an interior designer in a quality company in New York creating mostly designs for residency apartments for clients who shared my love for earth and nature. That success helped me acquire the financial level where I could make trips to fascinating destinations and explore the variety of earth's landscapes. It was during those trips that I found out the pleasure I get from travelling to such places and photographing what eventually other people enjoyed too. I started printing my favorite pictures and posting them to my social media accounts which led to an overwhelmingly high demand for print orders. I was sure then that this could mean a change in my career where I could combine happiness and pleasure for travelling and depicting nature's beauty through my lens.

Eventually I stopped working as an interior designer and gave all my energy in photography and acquiring a level of expertise in that field. It was the right choice for me and I don't look back despite my successful journey in interior designing.

Photography is surely a passion but it is only half of the big picture. The other half is nature with its captivating sceneries with mountains, rivers, lakes, forests and so many more that make me grateful to live in this planet. In these magical places during my journeys I came to find many interesting fragments of nature such as rocks and minerals, uniqely shaped wooden parts and beautiful leaves that I brought back with me as mementos. I started experimenting with those materials creating small jewelry and decorative artifacts for my home and as gifts for friends and through this proccess I ended up creating more useful items such as small furniture and clay cups and plates. So photography led me to another creative expression which is handcrafting with natural materials and being able to send them all around the globe. It is a fact that both interior design and photography need some technical knowledge of digital tools of trade which I had to master. As a result I am pretty confident and also enjoy a lot creating digital illustrations and 3D objects for satisfaction or as part of some collaborative project.

To conclude I would like to welcome you all embrace your inner instict and follow your true desires free of socially constructed burdens and obstacles because you all deserve the most out of your lives. Don't hesitate to make drastic changes not only in your career but in your everyday life. Trust yourself and remember... Creation Is Expression!
